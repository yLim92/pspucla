BECOMING A BROTHER

Requirements for Initiation

  • Attendance to at least 2 Rush events

  • Attendance to Bid Night (invitation only)

  • Pass a GPA check

  • Acceptance of the formal Bid

Requirements for Induction

  • Attendance to 2 out of 3 Initiate events

  • Attendance to weekly Initiate meetings

  • Pass all quizzes and the National Exam (80%)

  • Fundraising for our philanthropy Teach for America

  • One time Initiate dues of $145

  • $80 at Pinning Ceremony

  • (Active Brothers pay $65 per quarter at UCLA)
